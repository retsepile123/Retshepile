Traditional Basotho Music and Dance

Music and dance are central to Basotho culture. They express identity, storytelling, and communal unity.

Watch and Listen

Traditional Dances and Instruments

1. Mokhibo Dance

Performed by women kneeling gracefully while moving their shoulders to rhythm.

2. Mohobelo Dance

A powerful men’s dance featuring high kicks and synchronized stomping.

3. Ndlamo Dance

Inspired by warrior traditions, showing strength and bravery through movement.

4. Setapo

A playful circle dance accompanied by singing and clapping.

5. Thapo

A youth dance performed during initiation ceremonies.

6. Kiba

A communal dance that emphasizes rhythm and harmony among performers.

7. Mokorotlo Dance

Named after the famous hat, it celebrates leadership and unity.

8. Mokorotlo Chant

Used to welcome guests or leaders at special gatherings.

9. Lesiba Instrument

A traditional mouth-blown string instrument producing deep resonant sounds.

10. Thomo

A women’s musical bow played by plucking a string to create melodic tunes.

11. Sekhankula

A bowed instrument played by herders in the mountains.

12. Lipere-pere

A rhythmic dance that imitates the galloping of horses.

13. Pina ea Likhomo

A song dedicated to cattle, showing respect for livestock in Basotho culture.

14. Mokopu

A percussive gourd instrument that adds rhythm to traditional songs.

15. Lefela Dance

Performed by young men, emphasizing energy and physical agility.

16. Sekolo sa Mmino

Folk songs performed at cultural schools to teach traditional values.

17. Marabi Influence

Modern Basotho music influenced by early jazz styles.

18. Mokorotlo Drumming

Drums accompany ceremonial dances and royal gatherings.

19. Hae-Lesotho Dance

A storytelling dance that celebrates homeland pride.

20. Lifela Songs

Poetic chants performed by herders expressing wisdom and travel tales.

21. Lekopokopo

A social dance enjoyed at weddings and harvest celebrations.

22. Tšomo Music

Storytelling songs used to educate children about history and morals.

23. Lesotho Choirs

Powerful harmonies that combine traditional rhythms with spiritual messages.
